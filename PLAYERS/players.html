Top Ten Players

For the football lovers, this list consists of my top ten current players in world football, emphasis on the "MY" so some names may or may not surprise you. I really enjoy watching each player here for a lots of reasons. I personally enjoy players who are aesthetically pleasing to the eyes and for the Ronaldo fans, he isn't here. Enjoy!!

10

Erling Haaland

I love this guy so much I don't even know why. It still hurts that Chelsea won't be able to sign him anymore, instead we have Lakaka. Top finisher with insane pace, I remember my FM 17 days with him maybe that's why I like him so much Idk.

9

Mateo Kovacic

Kovacic's insane ball control and dribbling in tight spaces and under pressure is really rare amongst the world's current crop of midfielders and has improved a lot to his game over the years.


8

Bernado Silva

Always knew this guy was frightnening player, Pep has succesfully made a monster, when he gave Bernado a box to box role in that midfield. Coupled with his dribbling, shootinf and ball carrying ability he is a total menace to any team he faces.

7

Ngolo Kante

Next we have the most loved player in world football and the man behind Chelsea's recent successes. I don't have to go into details about how good this guy is. His reputation precedes him. Onto the next.


6

Karim Benzema

Another player who has been underrated over the years. Proper striker and one of the world's top finishers, finally getting the praise and recognition he deserves.

5

Robert Lewandowski

Lewagoalski, best finisher in world football right now, what am I saying best player in world football at the moment. Deserves a Balon'dor no cap, not the 2020 one tho, Robbed from it in 2019. but I think he's finally gonna get it, if he can keep this form coupled with a Champions League trophy.


4

Kevin De Bruyne

This guy always scores against us, mostly screamers I'm tired. I'm even having flashback's typing this. But he's another top player; outracious passing range, long shots with wonderful vision. He's just brilliant. Mourinho you no try honestly.

3

Mohammed Salah

If you still doubt Salah in 2022, there's no saving you. I was once like you though, I've seen the light. Since joining Liverpool he has taken his game to a higher level each year, World Class Player.


2

Kylian Mbappe

I've tried my best to hate this guy, but he's just that good. I remember his run against Argentina, it most be terrifying seeing Mbappe run at you with the ball, not much you can do. For me, he and Haaland are going to be dominating football once Ronaldo and Messi hang their boots.

1

Lionel Messi

Only guys who know ball will agree Messi is the best, I'm sorry it's just fact. What can't he do honestly; great passing, impeccable vision, dribbling no one comes close, goal scoring and lest I forget 7 Balon'dors My guy has done everything and ended that pointless arguement when he carried Argentina to the Copa America trophy. The only G.O.A.T

Is there anyone you think I missed?
