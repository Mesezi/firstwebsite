Top Ten Movies

The more I got into make this list, the more I realized I haven't seen a lot of movies. I'll have to fix that but my best advice would be, never use reviews to judge a movie until you've seen it yourself.

10

Legion

This was peak horror for me as a child. I don't think the movie is that popular but what I do remember is, it was about rapture, God sent angels to possess human's to kill a saviour baby that was yet to be born. Crazy. Angel Michael na rebel sha. He disobeyed God and saved the baby. In the end it was a "test" and Michael passed while Angel Gabriel failed. Lol.

9

Jexi

I love this movie, mostly because I relate a bit with the character, the whole akwardness and everything. Plus its really funny. It is all in all a pretty good movie and can be watched in whatever mood.


8

Tenet

Honestly I don't still understand this movie, I've watched countless breakdown's on Youtube but still don't grab everything. I just really enjoy the whole concept and the CGI when time runs backwards was really good. Na intellectual dey watch this kind movie.

7

Edge Of Tomorrow

Live. Die. Repeat. A fantastic movie full of action, suspense, and great characters. It starts out with a gripping scene that gives you a glimpse of what the movie is going to be about. Through the entire movie, the scenes keep your eyes on the screen with great CGI.


6

The Dark Knight Rises

Top notch movie. Acting, the storyline, cinematography and visual effects are perfect. Tom hardy was brilliant as Bane and sometimes I doubt if its him under the mask. The action and the scene with Bruce climbing out of banes prison was incredible.

5

Spider-Man
No way Home

Marvel keep on giving the fans what they want, seeing Tobey and Andrew coming back to take their roles was really nice. I was on the edge of my seat throught out the whole movie. You can't understand the joy we Spider-Man fans felt when Tobey and Andrew were introduced.


4

Django:Unchained

Jamie Foxx, Leonardo Dicaprio and Samuel L. Jackson, this movie was an absolute masterpiece.

3

Avengers: Endgame

This movie moved me to tears three times. That's a record. First when Natasha died, secondly when everyone that was dusted came back and finally Tony's death. Damn.


2

Deja Vu

A great movie for Denzel Washington fans to enjoy. Nice sci fi action thriller, great acting and a dramatic and fast paced movie

1

Inception

I've watched this movie so many times and it keeps getting better each time. There should be a statue of Leanordo Dicaprio somewhere. I still think he is dreaming tho.
